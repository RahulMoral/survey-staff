Purpose / 目的

Select a Company endeavors to be an organization that develops its people. We also like to have a workplace environment, culture and infrastructure that enable our people to perform their work well and in a meaningful and rewarading manner. 科思达教育集团私人有限公司立志要成为一个发展其员工的组织。我们也想有个适当的工作场所环境、 文化和基础架构，使我们的员工能够把工作做好、 做得有意义和有价值。

We would like to hear from you your views about the organization. The HR Function is conducting an internal climate survey to obtain your confidential feedback on the cultural and organizational characteristics of Select a Company. 我们想听取你对公司的意见。人力资源部门现正进行内部工作环境调研，征求您对于公司文化及组织特征的反馈及意见。

This survey should take about 20 minutes to complete. For this survey to be helpful and accurate, it is important that you answer each question as honestly as possible. 这项调查只需 20 分钟左右完成。为了确保调研是有用和准确，我们希望你尽可能真诚地回答每个问题。

Confidentiality / 保密

Please be assured that your responses to this survey will be kept completely anonymous. 我们会确保你对这项调研的反应完全匿名。

Please do not sign your name. The surveys will be administered, collated and analyzed solely by project team members. 请不要签署你的名字。这项调研完全由项目团队成员管理、 整理和分析。

Instructions / 说明
  • Please take your time to read each statement carefully. 请仔细阅读每个语句。
  • For each statement, decide how you feel about it accordingly to the possible alternatives. 每个语句，跟据所供给的选项决定你的感受。
  • Tick or circle one answer, e.g. 1, 2, 3 or 4, depending on how you feel. ✓ 或圈回答，例如 1、2、3或4。
  • Please do not miss out any statement. 请不要错过回答任何项目。
  • Please return your completed questionnaire to your supervisor within 1 week after you have received this questionnaire. 请您已完成的问卷在您收到这份问卷在1周内后交返你的主管。
